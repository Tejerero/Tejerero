Green Bean Salad

This simple, healthy and delicious green bean salad recipe goes well with any grilled meat or fish, and it's simple to prepare. It may be prepared ahead of time in big quantities, making it great for meal planning or preparing for dinner parties and festive holiday celebrations! It is best when marinated together overnight or much longer for better results.

From Chona's Kitchen by Chona Howard, August 11, 2020
Prep. Time

10 min.

Cooking Time

10 min.

Serving

4 Servings

Ingredients

196g green beens

122g sweet tomatoes (perino)

1 medium red onion

2-3 cloves garlic (chopped)

1/2 red bell pepper

1/2 cucumber

2 1/2 tbsp balsamic vinegar

1 1/2 olive oil

feta cheese (marinated)

2 tsp maple syrup

1-2 tsp honey

1/4 tsp black pepper

pinch of salt

Mixed dried fruits and nuts (Optional)

Instructions

step 1

In a cooking pot, boil water with a pinch of salt and when it boils add the raw green beans boil it for about 8 minutes or until tender-crisp. After 8 minutes transfer the green beans to a cold water, do not overcooked it. Then pat dry the beans with paper towel. Set Aside.

step 2

Cut the bellpeppers and cucumber in stripes. And cut the sweet tomatoes or perino in half. You can add other vegetables that suits your taste.

step 3

In a mixing bowl, make the dressing, just add the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, garlic, salt, pepper, maple syrup, and honey, mix until well-combined.

step 4

In a large mixing bowl, add the green beans and put a little bit of dressing, mix until well-combined. Then add all the vegetables and the marinated feta cheese.

step 5

Add the nuts and mixed dried fruits (Optional). Then add the remaining dressing. Mixed until well-combined.

step 6

Served and enjoy!
