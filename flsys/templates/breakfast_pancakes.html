Buttermilk Pancake

If you love making dishes from scratch, then this is the pancake recipe for you. You'll swear these are the BEST Fluffy Buttermilk Pancakes you've ever had! Every time you use this simple pancake recipe, you'll get very delicious and utterly wonderful pancakes! Much better than a store-bought mix! All you have to do is drizzle them with maple syrup and you're done.

From Yummy.ph by Yummy.ph Team, June 22, 2018
Prep. Time

20 min.

Cooking Time

15 min.

Serving

7 servings

Ingredients

PRICE OF INGREDIENT HERE

1 cup all-purpose flour

1 tablespoon sugar

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

1 1/4 cups buttermilk

1 large egg

2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

step 1

In a medium mixing bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, and baking soda. Pour in buttermilk, egg, and melted butter. Whisk just until mixed.

step 2

Over medium heat, heat a nonstick frying pan. Pour 1/3 cup batter into the center of the pan at a time. (Adjust the amount of batter to your desired pancake size.) Flip and cook the second side until the centers are firm but spring back when lightly touched when the tops begin to bubble, the edges are dry, and the bottom of the pancake is browned. Place the batter on a plate and repeat with the remaining batter.

step 3

Serve with butter and pancake syrup when still hot.
