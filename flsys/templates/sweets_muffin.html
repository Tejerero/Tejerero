Banana Chocolate Chip Muffins

These banana chocolate chip muffins are fluffy, delicious and perfect for kids snacks. This recipe will help you how to use up your never-ending stocks of overripe bananas. These muffins are golden, moist and bursting with banana flavor. They are perfect for breakfast, snack or dessert.

From The Taste of Kosher by Elissabeth
Prep. Time

10 min.

Cooking Time

20 min.

Serving

12 Muffins

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour (190 grams)

1 cup white sugar (200 grams)

1 teaspoon baking soda

1/2 teaspoon salt

3 medium bananas, mashed

1 egg

1/2 cup oil (120 milliliters)

1 teaspoon vanilla

1 cup semisweet chocolate chips (175 grams), plus more for sprinkling

Instructions

step 1

Preheat the oven to 350°F or 175°C.

step 2

In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt.

step 3

Add the mashed bananas, egg, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until everything is well combined. Then add the chocolate chips. Using a baking spatula mix until they are evenly distributed.

step 4

Spoon the batter into paper-lined standard 12-cup muffin tin. Sprinkle additional chocolate chips on each muffin cup.

step 5

Bake for 15 to 25 minutes* or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

step 6

Remove from the oven and set aside to cool for 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.
