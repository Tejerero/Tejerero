Tuna Salad Sandwich

Making tuna salad at home is one of the easiest and most satisfying meals you can make from your pantry ingredients. Start with these tuna salad recipe and enjoy it, just as it is, or customize it with your own favorite must-haves. It takes only a few minutes to prepare and is always handy when hunger strikes. You can serve it on toast, crackers, or with some vegetables like lettuce.

From Culinary Hill by Meggan Hill, May 10, 2021
Prep. Time

5 min.

Cooking Time

5 min.

Serving

6 Sandwiches

Ingredients

4 or 5 ounce cans tuna packed in oil/water drained

1 cup mayonnaise or less to taste

1/3 cup celery finely chopped (about 1 rib)

2 tablespoons red onion minced, about 2 small slices

2 tablespoon sweet pickle relish

1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

1 clove garlic minced

salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

step 1

In a medium bowl, combine tuna, mayonnaise, celery, onion, relish, lemon juice, and garlic.

step 2

Season to taste with salt and pepper (Recommendation: ½ teaspoons salt and ¼ teaspoon pepper).

step 3

Serve immediately or cover and chill until serving.
