Classic French Toast

French toast will never go out of style as a breakfast favourite. It reigns supreme among morning carbs, with its crispy, golden crust and creamy, fluffy interior. It's breakfast at its finest, with powdered sugar, maple syrup drizzled on top, and fresh fruit!

From allrecipes by Jan Bittner
Prep. Time

10 min.

Cooking Time

10 min.

Serving

2-3 servings

Ingredients

2 large eggs

1/4 cup heavy cream

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1/2 cup milk

1 1/2 tablespoons sugar

6-9 pieces of white bread

3 tablespoons butter

Semi-sweet chocolate chips (Optional)

Sliced fresh fruits for toppings

confectioner's sugar, sifted, to dust

Instructions

step 1

In a small bowl, whisk together the eggs, vanilla, cream, milk, and sugar. Soak the bread for 30 seconds to 1 minute per side in milk mixture.

step 2

In a large frying pan, melt 1Â½ tablespoons butter over medium heat. Add bread and sprinkle the top with chocolate chips (optional) cook for 1 to 2 minutes. Add the remaining butter to pan and flip the bread, cook for 1 to 2 more minutes or until golden brown.

step 3

On a serving platter, arrange the french toast in your preference. Top with any fresh fruits. Sprinkle with confectioners' sugar and drizzle with maple or pancake syrup. Serve while hot.
