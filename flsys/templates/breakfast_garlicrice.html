Garlic Fried Rice

Many Filipino households prepare sinangag on a regular basis. It's the garlic fried rice served with fried egg, sausages, daing na bangus, and other breakfast dishes. While the most basic version consists of toasted garlic tossed in day-old rice, Yummy.ph have discovered a better way to prepare garlic fried rice. It all depends on how the garlic is cooked.

From Yummy.ph by Roselle Miranda, April 24, 2021
Prep. Time

10 min.

Cooking Time

10 min.

Serving

4 servings

Ingredients

2 tablespoon oil

4 cloves of garlic (diced or smashed)

4 cups of day old rice

salt to taste

pepper to taste

chopped spring onion (Optional)

Instructions

step 1

Place your pot or large saute pan on the stove. In a cold oil, add roughly 2 tablespoons oil and 4 garlic cloves, diced or smashed.

step 2

Turn on the heat and set it to Low.

step 3

Allow the oil to heat up, then add the garlic and saut√© until it is golden brown in color.

step 4

Add the 4 cups of day-old rice to the pot. Season with salt and pepper to taste, garnish it with chopped spring onions and serve while hot.
