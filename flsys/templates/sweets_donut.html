Homemade Doughnuts

Homemade doughnuts are much easier than you would ever believe they are. You can make these fluffy and soft doughnuts at home with these easy recipe. These fluffy and soft doughnuts are what you want to eat when you're craving for something sweet. You can also get creative with the toppings, you can topped it with a chocolate glaze and sprinkles, or stick to the classic cinnamon-sugar coating. It's the doughnut you'll want to eat with a cup of coffee.

From Yummy.ph by Roselle Miranda, August 1, 2017
Prep. Time

10 min.

Cooking Time

35 min.

Serving

12 servings

Ingredients

3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

1 tablespoon instant dry yeast

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 cup water

1/4 cup sugar

1 egg

3 tablespoons butter, melted

canola oil, for frying

Instructions

step 1

In a mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, and yeast. Set aside.

step 2

On low speed, combine water, sugar, egg, and melted butter in a mixing bowl. Combine half of the flour mixture with the wet ingredients and stir until just combined. Mix in the remaining flour until everything is well mixed. Increase the speed to medium and continue to mix the dough, scraping down the sides as needed, for about 5 minutes, or until it is smooth.

step 3

Transfer dough to a large oiled bowl. Cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

step 4

Turn out onto a floured surface after removing the towel. Roll out the dough to a thickness of 1/4 inch. Cut doughnut shapes into the dough with a doughnut cutter or a 3-inch and 1-inch cutter, rerolling leftovers to form more doughnuts or holes. Place prepared doughnuts on a greased baking sheet until deep frying oil is ready.

step 5

Heat oil to 375?F in a deep saut√© pan. Place doughnuts in hot oil, 2-3 at a time. Use chopsticks or back of a wooden spoon to flip doughnuts over. Use slotted spoon for cooking the holes. Fry until golden brown on all sides.

step 6

Drain on paper towels. Serve doughnuts with your preferred filling or topping.
