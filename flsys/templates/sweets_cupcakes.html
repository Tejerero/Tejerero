Moist Chocolate Cupcakes

These moist chocolate cupcakes are made entirely from scratch and are bursting with chocolate flavor! The cupcake is so moist that it melts in your mouth. This Chocolate Cupcake recipe is quite simple to make, and the results are consistently delicious. At birthday parties, these are always the first dessert to vanish. This Chocolate Cupcake Recipe is a must-try for everyone who like homemade cupcakes.

From Natasha's Kitchen by Natasha Kravchuk
Prep. Time

10 min.

Cooking Time

20 min.

Serving

12 Cupcakes

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

1 cup granulated sugar

1/3 cup natural unsweetened cocoa

1 tsp baking soda

1/2 tsp salt

1 cup warm coffee, or 8 fluid oz

1 tbsp white distilled vinegar

1/3 cup olive oil or vegetable oil

1 large egg, room temperature

2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

step 1

Preheat the oven to 350Â°F and place the rack in the middle. Fill a 12-count muffin tray with cupcake liners.

step 2

Mix the dry ingredients thoroughly in a large mixing bowl, breaking up most of the cocoa clumps.

step 3

In a second mixing bowl, whisk together wet ingredients.

step 4

Pour the wet ingredients over the dry ingredients and whisk just until everything is combined and there are no dry streaks. The batter will be a little lumpy and loose.

step 5

Fill each cup 2/3 full with batter using an ice cream scoop (do not over-fill). Bake for 18-22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean (20 minutes was perfect). Cool for 5 minutes in the pan before transferring to a cooling rack to cool completely before frosting.
