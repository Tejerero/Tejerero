Chicken Adobo

This recipe is quick, simple, and easy. This is the simple adobo recipe you'll want to learn and master if you're a newbie cook. Chicken adobo is a Filipino dish made with chicken slices, marinated with soy sauce and seasonings, which are then pan-fried and simmered till soft. The dish gained popularity because of its delicious taste and ease of preparation.

From Panlasang Pinoy by Vanjo Merano
Prep. Time

10 min.

Cooking Time

40 min.

Serving

4 servings

Ingredients

2 lbs chicken cut into serving pieces

3 pieces dried bay leaves

8 tablespoons soy sauce

4 tablespoons white vinegar

5 cloves garlic crushed

1 teaspoon whole black pepper

1 1/2 cups water

3 tablespoons cooking oil

1 tablespoon sugar

2 pieces sliced potato (optional)

Instructions

step 1

In a large mixing bowl, combine the chicken, soy sauce, and garlic. Mix thoroughly. For at least 1 hour, marinate the chicken. The longer the duration, the better.

step 2

Heat a cooking pan. Pour cooking oil.

step 3

Allow the oil to heat up, pan-fry the marinated chicken for 2 minutes per side once the oil is heated enough.

step 4

Pour the remaining marinade, including the garlic, into the pan. Add the water. Bring the water to boil

step 5

Add dried bay leaves and whole black pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.

step 6

Add in the vinegar, let it simmer for 5 minutes. Then stir.

step 7

Add the sugar, stir and turn off the stove. Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!
